Our collection will consist of 5,000 MooPals serving as digital collectibles, each with a unique twist. Every MooPal will possess its distinct characteristics, ensuring its exclusivity to you. We have thoughtfully designed a color palette that aligns with our brand, ensuring a delightful, vibrant, and adorable appearance. By acquiring a MooPal, you'll have your own charming companion to represent you on various platforms. Furthermore, you'll become part of our fantastic community and accompany us as we mooove through both the physical and digital realms.

A Pal for everyone!

As mentioned earlier, we meticulously crafted the colour palette to impart a welcoming atmosphere throughout the collection, something that can resonate with everyone. This same principle applies to the traits we've designed. We've taken great care to encompass a diverse range of interests, enabling a broader appeal to various audiences. Your ideal MooPal awaits you!

How are MooPals made?

Every trait belonging to MooPals has been thoughtfully crafted by the artist, Nosta. His remarkable ability to maintain a simplistic aesthetic while paying close attention to detail shines through in each design. Each MooPal is generated by merging our vast array of over 150 traits, ensuring that every collectible possesses its unique characteristics. P.S. They all have the same distinctive MOO!

Rarity

While each MooPal is unique, some are more rare than others! That is because the traits that come together to generate a MooPal all have different levels of rarity. Some traits are common and show up in the collection more often, while rare traits only exist on a few pals. The more rare traits you have on your pal, the more rare your pal is overall making it extra special!

1 of 1 Pals

We have a total of 15 pals that are “1 of 1” pals in the collection. These are totally unique, hand-drawn pals that do not share any traits with any other pal in the collection. These are considered the most rare and desirable in the collection.
