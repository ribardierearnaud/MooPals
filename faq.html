Frequently Asked Questions

What is an NFT?

An NFT, or Non-Fungible Token, is a unique digital asset that represents ownership or proof of authenticity of a digital item. Unlike cryptocurrencies like Bitcoin or Ethereum, NFTs cannot be exchanged on a one-to-one basis because each NFT is distinct and has its own value.
